  1. Click the Move tool (‚úã)

Moving Annotations

  1. Click the Move tool (‚úã)

üí° Tips & Tricks

üéØ Precise Annotations

Use the zoom feature (Ctrl + +) to zoom in for precise annotation placement on small UI elements.

üîí Protecting Privacy

Always use the Redact tool (‚¨õ) to hide sensitive information like passwords, API keys, or personal data before submitting.

üìù Clear Communication

Use the Text tool (T) to add numbered steps or descriptions directly on the screenshot. This helps developers understand the issue context.

üé¨ Effective Video Recording

Before recording, rehearse the steps to reproduce the issue. This creates cleaner, more focused videos.

üé® Color Coding

üì∏ Multiple Screenshots

‚Ü∂ Don't Fear Mistakes

‚úã Quick Edit Workflow

When using the Move tool, selected annotations show a blue dashed box and stay selected after moving. This lets you quickly reposition and delete annotations without re-selecting them. Press Delete right after moving to remove an unwanted annotation.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I edit annotations after creating them?

A: Yes! Use the Move tool (‚úã) to select annotations (a blue selection box will appear). You can then drag to reposition them or press Delete/Backspace to remove them. Selected annotations remain selected after moving, making it easy to delete them if needed.

Q: How do I record audio with my video?

A: Currently, Cap Screen records tab audio automatically when recording tabs. Screen recordings capture system audio.

Q: What happens to my recordings after I submit?

A: Recordings are saved according to your configured destination (e.g., uploaded to your issue tracking system).

Q: Can I save my work without submitting?

A: Your work is automatically saved in the current session. However, closing the tab will discard unsaved reports.

Q: Why is my recording toolbar transparent?

A: The toolbar is designed to be minimally intrusive during recording. Hover over it to see full details and controls.
