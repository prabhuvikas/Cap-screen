â“ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I edit annotations after creating them?

A: Yes! Use the Move tool (âœ‹) to select and reposition annotations. You can delete them with Delete/Backspace.

Q: How do I record audio with my video?

A: Currently, Cap Screen records tab audio automatically when recording tabs. Screen recordings capture system audio.

Q: What happens to my recordings after I submit?

A: Recordings are saved according to your configured destination (e.g., uploaded to your issue tracking system).

Q: Can I save my work without submitting?

A: Your work is automatically saved in the current session. However, closing the tab will discard unsaved reports.

Q: Why is my recording toolbar transparent?

A: The toolbar is designed to be minimally intrusive during recording. Hover over it to see full details and controls.
